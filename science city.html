SCIENCE CITY

Gujarat Science City, located in Hebatpur, Ahmedabad, is a part of Gujarat government's initiative to draw more students towards education in science. The center hosts an IMAX 3D theatre, an energy park, a hall of science, Planet Earth, an amphitheatre, Life Science Park and dancing musical fountains among others.



Address: Science City Rd, Off, Sarkhej - Gandhinagar Hwy, Ahmedabad, Gujarat 380060
Purpose: Education
